Welcome to IRCtm

23 May 2016

After a bit of wait, the IRCtm official website is officially here! Throughout development, we have committed ourselves to providing you with everything you should need to make use of our IRC network.

In development of the site we figured that adding a blog would be a worthwhile addition. IRCtm is a server that is constantly continuing to improve, so to document these improvements, we have resorted to maintaining a blog. Here you will find everything we have done to improve the chat server, as well as find news on services we may begin development on in the future.

Here is a documentation of what we've added to the site for your own convenience.

  • Web Client - If you are not comfortable using your own downloadable IRC client, we are happy to announce that we have embedded our own web-based client to the site. Using this, you can chat with your friends without the burden of being forced to use third party client.
  • Rules - To help prevent problems in the future, we have provided a rules page to the site, allowing users to be able to easily view the server rules before beginning to chat.
  • Premium Hosting - Have you ever dreamed of free, unlimited SSD hosting? It is time to stop dreaming. With permission, IRCtm members are able to access premium hosting, decked out with the latest CPanel distribution as well as premium addons like Softaculous.
  • Responsiveness - Our site is completely responsive, meaning that it can be used on your tablet, phone, and other devices without the site design being compromised.

Once again, we sincerely wish you are able to make good use of the site. Hope to see you on the chat server!
