ZNC Bouncer Configurations

Configurating a ZNC Bouncer is a worthwhile use of your time. Using this service allows you to always be logged onto IRC.tm, without missing messages when you close your chat client. This service is critical for those who enjoy catching up on what has previously been said on the chat server. With this program set up, you will always be 'in the loop'.

To configure your ZNC Bouncer, contact John on the IRC.tm chat server. He will provide you with your own ZNC account, as well as provide you with information that will help you get the most out of your chat bouncer.
